The Idea

The concept for AeroLeaf was born in a small apartment in a crowded city. We noticed that despite our best efforts, the indoor air felt stale, and traditional mechanical purifiers were noisy and sterile.

We asked ourselves: "What if nature could be supercharged by technology?"

By collaborating with botanists and hardware engineers, we discovered a way to enhance the natural filtering power of root microbiomes, while providing beautiful, visual feedback. After 4 years of R&D, AeroLeaf was born—a living, breathing piece of technology.
